Tonkotsu Ramen

This is my attempt on ramen and it turned out excellent! So, this is my take on Joshua Weissman and Way of Ramen "Tonkotsu Ramen" with my twist.

Broth:
  • 3lbs pig trotters
  • 1 bunch green onion
  • 1 LG yellow onion
  • 2 shallots
  • 4 garlic cloves
Chashu:
  • 2lbs pork belly
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup mirin
  • 1 cup sake
  • 2 inch sliced ginger
  • 1 bunch green onion
  • 4-5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
Tare:
  • 3/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup + 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp msg
  • optional: 1/4 cup chashu braising liquid

Mac n Cheese

Here's a request from my colleagues.

Ingredients:
  • 1/2 box pasta: your choice
  • Heavy Whipping Cream(optional)
  • Milk
  • 1 cup Sharp Cheddar
  • 16oz Velveeta Cheese
  • Chicken Bouillon
Italian Seasoning Mix:
  • 1/4 tsp Italian Seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Garlic Powder
  • optional: 1/4 cup chashu braising liquid
The Process:
  1. Cook your pasta, however done you want. I usually use a cube of Chicken Bouillon or tbsp of the granular one in the water.
  2. When the pasta is done, save some of the water broth about a 1/4 cup and simmer with 1/4 cup of milk or heavy whipping cream.
  3. Milk mix should bubble slightly and then add half a cup of sharp cheddar and whisk until smooth, then add 2 oz of velveeta.
  4. If cheese sauce is too thick add a little bit of water and mix.
  5. Now add in the italian seasoning mix
  6. Add pasta and mix!
  7. Note: You may want to save some more broth just incase you want the cheese sauce runnier. Oh, keep an eye one the water if you add bouillon cause it'll foam over.
