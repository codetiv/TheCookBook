Dough Recipe

The basic for most baking recipe whether it be pizza, bagel, or bread. This is what my fundamental ingredients are:

  • 3 cups Flour
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 1/4tsp yeast or 1 packet
  • 1 1/4 cup warm water(90-115*F)
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil(optional)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Sugar

First you start off with getting your yeast prep first in warm water, wait roughly 10min. While waiting grab your dry ingredients and mix in a bowl. Once the yeast is done add to the dry mix and...MIX! When I started kneading I use to put some oomph to it and that not necessary. Use some amount of effort and get it to form a nice smooth dough ball, give it a stretch to see if you can see some light through it. Put it in an oil bowl, wait for 1 hour or put it in the fridge til tomorrow!
